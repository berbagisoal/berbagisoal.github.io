20. Kata" yang di pakai dalam expressing obligation


Materi: Expressing obligation

Soal: Kata-kata yang dipakai


Peyelesaian:

Kata-kata yang dipakai dalam expressing obligation, yaitu:

1. Must

2. Have to

3. Should

Things to remember:

1.The difference between "Have to" and "must" is that "have to"indicates that someone else has imposed conditions on us.

2. The simple past of "must" is "had to".

3. The simple future of "must" is "will have to".

4. Should also expresses obligation. But there is a difference between must / have to and should:

must / have to = 100% obligation. "When the traffic light turn red, you must stop."

should = 50% obligation. It's more an advice than an obligation. " You look tired. you should have a rest."

25. pengertian expressing obligation, contohnya, strukturnya dan tenses yg dipakai dalam bahasa indonesia


1. Menggunakan Adjectives: Obligatory, Compulsory, Needless, Unnecessary

Contoh Kalimat:
It is obligatory for the people to participate in education.
(Artinya: Suatu keharusan bagi orang-orang untuk berpartisipasi dalam pendidikan)
Entering elementary school is compulsory in Indonesia.
(Artinya: Masuk Sekolah Dasar merupakan keharusan di Negara Indonesia)
That’s a needless thing to speak with him.
(Artinya: Taka da gunanya berbicara dengan dia)
It’s unnecessary to sit under the tree for hours.
(Artinya: Tidak perlu duduk di bawah pohon selama berjam-jam)
Etc.

2. Menggunakan Nouns: Obligation, Compulsion, Need, Necessity

Contoh Kalimat:
There’s a legal obligation to enter the army.
(Artinya: Ada keharusan resmi untuk masuk Angkatan Bersenjata)
It’s compulsion to obey your parents.
(Artinya: Merupakan keharusan untuk mematuhi orang tuamu)
It’s a need for everybody to eat nutritious food.
(Artinya: Perlu bagi setiap orang untuk makan makanan yang bergizi)
Is it necessity for man and woman to sit apart?
(Artinya: Apakah perlu bagi laki-laki dan wanita untuk duduk berpisah?)

3. Menggunakan Verbs: Oblige

Contoh Kalimat:
My father obliged me to study every night.
(Artinya: Ayah saya mewajibkanku untuk belajat setiap malam)

4. Menggunakan Modal Auxiliaries: Must, Have to, Ought to, Should

Contoh Kalimat:
You must obey your parents.
(Artinya: Kamu harus mematuhi kedua orang tuamu)
Every Muslim must pray five times a day.
(Artinya: Setiap umat Islam harus sholat lima kali sehari)
I have to go now.
(Artinya: Saya harus pergi sekarang)
You ought to repair my watch.
(Artinya: Kamu harus memperbaiki jamku)
Those shoes should be repaired.
(Artinya: Sepatu itu seharusnya diperbaiki)
I ought to wake up early.
(Artinya: Saya harus bangun pagi)
Etc.

28. kak tolong bantuin dong☺ buatkan contoh kalimat (masing" satu) dari expressing probability, expressing ability, expressing obligation and advice, expressing permission, dan expressing habits. terima kasih☺☺??


1. (Kemungkinan) Probability : "Maybe I could help you to service this software in your laptop. Because I know enough a lot about this software." Yang digaris bawahi ini sebagai kalimat pendukung untuk membuktikan bahwa kita mampu dan sekaligus meyakinkan orang.

2. (Kemampuan) Ability : "I can to completing the difficult task of writing this poem. So, I shall to completing it... Poetry is my specialization." Yang digaris bawahi ini juga sebagai kalimat pendukung dan meyakinkan, baik diri sendiri maupun orang lain 

3. (Kewajiban) Obligation : "As an obedient Muslim, I obliged to carry out the task of the five daily prayers of My Lord since birth." (Sebagai seorang Muslim yang taat, aku wajib melaksanakan tugas shalat lima waktu dari Tuhan-ku sejak lahir.)
    (Nasihat) Advice : "As your teacher, I suggest you to learn a lot more for the semester in Mathematics.. for the good of yourself." (Sebagai gurumu, aku sarankan bahwa kau harus belajar lebih banyak untuk Matematika... demi kebaikan dirimu sendiri.)

4. (Izin) Permission : "Well, I am going to give you permission to let out this room. For 2 minutes."

5. (Kebiasaan) Habits : "Yeah, boys. Reading has become my habit, since during my mother pregnancy of me; she diligently reading for me."

SEMOGA MEMBANTU~1) Probability (kemungkinan) : Maybe, this day will be a rainy day because the sky is cloudy
2) Ability (kemampuan) : I can swimming because i try hard
3) Obligation (kewajiban) : As a good student, i must study every day
4) Advice (nasihat) : You did better do your homework before you are too lazy to do it anymore
5) Permission (ijin) : okay, i will give you my permission to go out with your friend
6) Habits (kebiasaan) : I usually go to school at morning
