Explanation:

Asking suggestion is a type of expression which is used when someone asks for an advice or recommendation from another person.
Here are some expressions that you can use to ask for a suggestion:
- Could you give me an advice?
- What should ...?
- Any idea?
- Do you have any suggestions?
- What do you recommend ...?

Giving suggestion is a type of expression which is used when you want to give an advice or recommendation for someone.
Here are some expressions that you can use:
- You should ...
- You ought to ...
- It's better for you to ...
- I suggest you ...
- I advise you to ...

17. which word indicate the expressions of giving suggestion or advice


Suggestions and Advice in English

Kamu dapat menggunakan kata-kata di bawah ini dalam sebuah percakapan untuk menunjukan atau memberikan saran kepada orang lain.


Suggestions

Should

"You should try to practise English." "You shouldn't translate too much."

Why don't you

"Why don't you join an English club?""Why don't you take a seat next to me?"

Ought to

"You ought to read more.""You ought to go out once in a while."

If I were you, I'd...

"If I were you, I'd watch more television.""If I were you, I'd be in the cloud nine"

Semua contoh kalimat diikuti kata kerja, tanpa to. Contohnya: "He should visit the Eiffel Tower." (Bukan "he should to visit the Eiffel Tower.")

Suggest and Recommend (verb+ing)

"I suggest visiting the Eiffel Tower.""I recommend eating in the cheap restaurant"Advice

Advise (verb)

"I advise you to buy a good dictionary." (Pengucapannya ad - vaiz)"I advise you not to go."

Advice (noun)

"Can you give me some advice?" (Pengucapannya ad - vais)"Let me give you some advice.""She gave me a very useful piece of advice: to buy a good dictionary."

Advice adalah uncountable noun. Yang artinya kita tidak dapat menyebut sebagai an advice. Tetapi penggunaan yang benar adalah some advice or a piece of advice.
