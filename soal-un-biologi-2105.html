x + 1/x = 7

x⁴ + 1/x⁴ = [(x + 1/x)² - 2 . x . 1/x]² - 2(x . 1/x)²

x⁴ + 1/x⁴ = [7² - 2]² - 2

x⁴ + 1/x⁴ = 47² - 2

x⁴ + 1/x⁴ = 2207

P = 20 cm

L = 11 cm

T = 9 cm

Volume akhir = 2105cm^3

Ditanya

panjang sisi kubus yang di celupkan = ??cm

Penyelesaian

Vbalok = P x L x T
