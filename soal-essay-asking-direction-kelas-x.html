10. pengertian asking n giving direction


asking and giving direction :
bila diartikan per kata menjadi : bertanya dan memberikan petunjuk
asking and giving direction banyak digunakan untuk menjelaskan letak sebuah tempat
example :
A : Where is the police station?
B : If you want to go to the police station just go a head (jalan lurus/lurus terus) until you see the t-junctions (pertigaan) u have to turn right (belok kanan) and go a head again, and the police station is across (di sebrang jalan) the bakery.
A : Thanks!
B : You're welcome!

Don't do this! :
A : Where is the police station?
B : You have to find the taxi! and just ask the taxi where is the police station and he will take you to the police station.
