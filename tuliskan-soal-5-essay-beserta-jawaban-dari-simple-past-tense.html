10. Tulis rumus simple past tense dan simple present tense Beserta Contohnya


Simple past tense:

subject + V2 + O

ex:
(+) I went to primary school in England.
(-) My grandmother didn't like cats.
(?) Did you get my letter?


Simple present tense:

Subject + V(use s at the back if the subject are he, she, it) + O

ex:
(+) She wears jeans all the time.
(-) He doesn't enjoy it.
(?) Does Jack smoke?@Answer:

1) Simple Present
(+) S + V1(s/es) + O/C => She reads a book.
(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + O/C => She doesn't read a book.
(?) Do/does + S + V1 + O/C? => Does she read a book?

2) Simple Past
(+) S + V2 + O/C => We cook a cake.
(-) S + did + not + V1 + O/C => We didn't cook a cake.
(?) Did + S + V1 + O/C? => Did we cook a cake?

20. 1.tuliskan rumus nominal simple past tense(+), (-), (?)2.tuliskan rumus verbal simple past tense(+), (-), (?)3.tuliskan rumus noun simple present tense(+), (-), (?)4.tuliskan rumus verb simple present tense(+), (-), (?)5.tuliskan rumus continuous simple past tense(+), (-), (?)6.tuliskan rumus simple past tense(+), (-), (?)7.tuliskan rumus continuous simple past tense(+), (-), (?)8.tuliskan rumus present perfect simple past tense n simple present tense(+), (-), (?)9.tuliskan rumus future simple past tense n present tense(+), (-), (?)10.tuliskan rumus infinitive and modals simple past tense n present tense(+), (-), (?)bantu jawab please​


Jawaban:

1. Rumus nominal simple past tense:

(+) Subject + verb (ed) + object

(-) Subject + did not + verb (base form) + object

(?) Did + subject + verb (base form) + object?

Contoh:

(+) She danced last night.

(-) She did not dance at the party.

(?) Did she dance with him?

2. Rumus verbal simple past tense:

(+) Subject + verb (base form) + object

(-) Subject + did not + verb (base form) + object

(?) Did + subject + verb (base form) + object?

Contoh:

(+) He sang a song last night.

(-) He did not sing in the talent show.

(?) Did he sing well?

3. Rumus noun simple present tense:

(+) Subject + verb (s/es) + object

(-) Subject + do/does not + verb (base form) + object

(?) Do/does + subject + verb (base form) + object?

Contoh:

(+) She writes a letter every day.

(-) She does not write emails.

(?) Does she write in cursive?

4. Rumus verb simple present tense:

(+) Subject + verb (base form) + object

(-) Subject + do/does not + verb (base form) + object

(?) Do/does + subject + verb (base form) + object?

Contoh:

(+) He loves pizza.

(-) He does not eat spicy food.

(?) Does he cook often?

5. Rumus continuous simple past tense:

(+) Subject + was/were + verb (ing) + object

(-) Subject + was/were not + verb (ing) + object

(?) Was/were + subject + verb (ing) + object?

Contoh:

(+) She was dancing at the party.

(-) She was not singing on stage.

(?) Were they talking about me?

6. Rumus simple past tense:

(+) Subject + verb (ed) + object

(-) Subject + did not + verb (base form) + object

(?) Did + subject + verb (base form) + object?

Contoh:

(+) They visited their grandparents last weekend.

(-) They did not travel abroad last summer.

(?) Did he finish his project on time?

7. Rumus continuous simple past tense:

(+) Subject + was/were + verb (ing) + object

(-) Subject + was/were not + verb (ing) + object

(?) Was/were + subject + verb (ing) + object?

Contoh:

(+) She was dancing at the party.

(-) She was not singing on stage.

(?) Were they talking about me?

8. Rumus present perfect simple past tense dan simple present tense:

(+) Subject + have/has + verb (past participle) + object

(-) Subject + have/has not + verb (past participle) + object

(?) Have/has + subject + verb (past participle) + object?

Contoh:

(+) He has traveled to many countries.

(-) She has not finished her homework yet.

(?) Have they seen this movie before?

9. Rumus future simple past tense dan present tense:

(+) Subject + will + verb (base form) + object

(-) Subject + will not + verb (base form) + object

(?) Will + subject + verb (base form) + object?

Contoh:

(+) They will meet at the park tomorrow.

(-) She will not attend the meeting.

(?) Will he arrive on time?

10. Rumus infinitive and modals simple past tense dan present tense:

(+)Subject + modal (can/could/may/might/will/would/should) + verb (base form) + object

(-) Subject + modal (can't/couldn't/may not/might not/will not/would not/should not) + verb (base form) + object

(?) Modal (can/could/may/might/will/would/should) + subject + verb (base form) + object?

Contoh:

(+) I can swim in the pool.

(-) They could not finish the project on time.

(?) May I borrow your book?

ᴥ Fungsi

Mengungkapkan suatu peristiwa/kegiatan yang sedang terjadi atau berlangsung di masa yang telah berlalu. Misalnya: Saya sedang menulis berita saat kamu datang kemarin.

ᴥ Pattern:

(+) Subject + was/were + Verb-ing + Object/Complement(–) Subject + was/were + not + Verb-ing + Object/Complement(?) Was/Were + Subject + Verb-ing + Object/Complement + ?

ᴥ Notes:

Was, digunakan pada subject: I, he, she, dan it. Were, digunakan pada subject: You, we, dan they.

40. tuliskan contoh dari simple past tense dan simple present tense minimal 5​


Jawaban:

Simple past tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan yang dilakukan di waktu lampau.

Simple past tense terbagi menjadi dua, yaitu:

nominal past tense (kalimat yang tidak membutuhkan kata kerja)

Rumusnya:

subject + was/were + noun

subject + was/were + not + noun

was/were + subject + noun?

verbal past tense (kalimat yang membutuhkan kata kerja)

Rumusnya:

subject + v2

subject + did not + v1

did + subject + v1?

Ciri simple past tense adalah adanya keterangan waktu lampau, seperti yesterday, ago, last.

(-) They were not here two hours ago.

(?) Were they here two hours ago?

Yes, they were. / No, they were not.

(-) We didn't watch a football match yesterday.

(-) She didn't drink a glass of milk this morning.

(?) Did she drink a glass of milk this morning?
