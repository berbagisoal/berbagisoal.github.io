26. Mention 5 expressions of agreement! Mention 5 expressions of disagreement!


5 expressions of agreement are:

1. I couldn't agree more, the school must change the regulation immediately.

2. I agree with you, a student must read books a lot.

3. That's right, this park is so beautiful.

4. You are absolutely right, we must go to her house now.

5. He is right, the birthday party was awesome.  

5 expressions of disagreement are:

1. I respectfully disagree with you, you see, this matter can be explained in several ways.

2. I'm afraid I have to disagree with you there, because this thing is debatable.

3. Wait a minute, that's not true, I never did such thing.

4. I totally disagree, it's illegal you know that.

5. I disagree, you can't do such thing.

Pembahasan

Di dalam soal di atas kita diminta untuk memberikan beberapa kalimat yang menggambarkan persetujuan (agreement) dan ketidaksetujuan (disagreement). Di dalam 5 bagian kalimat yang pertama tentang agreement kita punya 5 cara mudah untuk memberikan persetujuan misalnya:

1. I couldn't agree more...

2. I agree with you...

3. That's right...

4. You are absolutely right...

5. He is right...

Lalu kita lanjutkan dengan kalimat yang menjelaskan bagaimana sisi kita dalam persetujuan tersebut seperti dalam contoh jawaban di atas.

Bagian kedua adalah tentang disagreement di antaranya:

1. I respectfully disagree with you...

2. I'm afraid I have to disagree with you there...

3. Wait a minute, that's not true...

4. I totally disagree...

5. I disagree...
