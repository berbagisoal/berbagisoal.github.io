Penjelasan:

ASKING FOR INFORMATION

Untuk menunjukkan ekspresi asking for information, kita dapat menggunakan kalimat tanya 5W+H yaitu, who, what, where, when, whom dan how atau bisa juga dengan auxiliary verb seperti do, did, does, can, could dan lain sebaganya. Berikut contoh Asking for Information:

Do you know where the post office is?Could you explain to me about this project?Who is the manager of this store?Why are you here?When did they leave this city?How do you finish this homework?Can you tell me about your passion?What are you doing here?How many people are there in the concert?Do you see my English book?

GIVING INFORMATION

Berikut contoh kalimat untuk memberikan informasi/giving information expression:

It's on Jalan Harupat No.3, if you see a tall orange building, that's the post office.His father is a pilot.I am doing my homework now.The concert is on Saturday night.Mr. Charlie has five children.I saw your English book in your locker.There are 12 people attend the meeting.The train is leaving at 12.30 PM.I am here because I heard the news on TV.It's 9 PM.Pelajari Lebih Lanjut

24. contoh dialog asking giving service & asking giving things


Kelas : VII
Pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
kategori : expressions
kata kunci : asking giving service, asking giving things

pembahasan:
Asking and giving service expressions adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk memberikan pelayanan. Sedangkan asking and giving things expressions adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk memberikan sesuatu kepada seseorang.

Contoh dialog asking and giving service:
You : Good morning, Ma'am.
Your teacher : Good morning.
You : Yesterday I heard about English story telling contest and I would like to join it. Where do I get the form, Ma'am? (asking for service)
Your teacher : Oh I see. Let me help you. (giving service)
You : Thank you, Ma'am. 
Your teacher : Don;t forget to give your parent's sign on the form.
You : Yes, Ma'am. 

contoh dialog asking and giving things:
You : Hi, can you help me? (asking for help)
Your friend : What can I do for you?
You : I lost my pen. May I lend your pencil? (asking for something)
your friend : Of course. Here it is. (responding of giving something)
You : And... one more. Do you have an English dictionary? May I lend it too? asking for something)
Your friend : I'm sorry. I use it now.(refusing for giving something)
You: It's okay. Thank you.
Your friend : Your welcome.
