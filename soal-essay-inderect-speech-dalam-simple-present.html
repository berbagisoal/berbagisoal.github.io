2. contoh kalimat direct speech present simple dan indirecht speech past simple


Hi, Ayu.

Reported Speech atau yang bisa dibagi jadi Direct atau Indirect Speech itu selalu ada perubahan yang tersusun. Mungkin nanti bisa belajar lagi tentang perubahan tenses-nya.

Contoh:
Direct Speech dalam Present Tense:
"I usually read a book before going to sleep," he said.

Karena present maka harus diubah menjadi Indirect Speech yang harus dalam Past Tense dengan alasan karena si penutur menyampaikan sesuatu yang disampaikan oleh penutur yang lain atau dalam kata lain sudah terjadi.

Indirect Speech dalam Past Tense:
He told me (atau He said) that he usually read a book before going to sleep.

"I" diubah menjadi "he" dan read (present tense) diubah menjadi read (past tense).
*PS: Karena read tidak mengalami perubahan bentuk dalam past tense maka bentuk penulisan tetap tapi memiliki perbedaan di pengucapan. Read dalam present tense dibaca 'rid' sedang dalam past tense dibaca 'red' seperti membaca kata "red" (warna merah).

Hope it helps.

6. buatlah kalimat reported speech dengan tenses simple present tense 5 kalimat, present perfect 5 kalimat


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: reported speech
Pembahasan: 

In reported speech, present simple and perfect are not used. Only two tenses are used in the reported speech, past simple and past perfect. 

Key in reported speech tense shift: 
- present simple becomes past simple.
- present perfect becomes past perfect
- past simple becomes past perfect

present simple:                                              reported speech
- She said, 'I know you.'                    - She said that she knew me.
                  'I'm always with  you.'      - She said that she was always with me.
                  'I care about you.'            - She said that she cared about me.
                  'I'm here.'                         - She said that she was here.
                  'I'm ready to help.'           - She said that she was ready to help.

present perfect simple:
- He said ,'I've got her.'                     - He said that he had got her.
               ,'I've made up my mind.'   - He said that he had made up my mind.
                'I've been there.'               - He said that he had been there.
                'I've had it.'                        - He said that he had had it.
                'I've called her.'                 - He said that he had called her.

Penjelasan :

KHUSUS UNTUK PRESENT SIMPLE

V1 berubah menjadi V2
• He says, "I feel happy."
menjadi : He said that he felt happy.

"Don't" dan "Doesn't" menjadi "Didn't"
• She says, "I don't like cucumbers."
menjadi : She said she didn't like cucumbers.

"Can" menjadi "Could", "Can't" menjadi "Couldn't"
• He says to me, "I can swim."
menjadi : He told me that he could swim.

CATATAN :
Kalau kalimatnya masih benar sampai sekarang, tidak perlu mengubah tense.
• My uncle says, "I go to work every day."
kalau paman masih bekerja setiap hari sampai sekarang, tetap menjadi :
My uncle said that he goes to work every day.

1. They are working too hard today, do you see it? - Di sini kalimat menunjukkan bahwa mereka (mereka yang malas) sebenarnya sedang bekerja keras. Frase "are working" mengindikasikan bahwa tindakan bekerja sedang berlangsung pada saat ini.

2. I am giving her a flower now. - Kalimat ini menunjukkan bahwa pembicara (I) sedang memberikan bunga kepada seseorang (her) pada saat ini. "Am giving" menunjukkan bahwa tindakan memberi sedang berlangsung pada saat ini.

3. Anita is doing the examination today, pray for her success. - Frase "is doing" menunjukkan bahwa saat ini Anita sedang melakukan ujian. Kalimat ini juga meminta untuk mendoakan keberhasilan Anita dalam ujian tersebut.

4. You are making a lot of noise, could you be quieter? - Kalimat ini mengatakan bahwa "you" sedang membuat banyak kebisingan pada saat ini. Permintaan "could you be quieter?" menunjukkan bahwa pembicara meminta agar "you" menjadi lebih tenang.

5. What are you doing in this place? - Pertanyaan ini menanyakan apa yang "you" (yang sedang di dalam tempat ini) sedang lakukan saat ini. "Are you doing" menunjukkan bahwa pembicara ingin mengetahui tindakan yang sedang berlangsung.

6. Is Sabrina working today? - Pertanyaan ini menanyakan apakah Sabrina sedang bekerja hari ini. "Is working" menunjukkan bahwa tindakan bekerja untuk Sabrina sedang berlangsung pada saat ini.

9. When am I going today? - Pertanyaan ini menanyakan kapan pembicara akan pergi pada hari ini. "Am going" menunjukkan bahwa tindakan pergi akan dilakukan oleh pembicara pada saat ini.

27. What are the differencies between derect and inderect speech


Direct speech can be used in virtually every tense in English. You can use it to describe something in the present tense – to express something that is happening in the present moment, or make it feel like it is happening right now.

For example:

“While she’s on the phone, she’s saying to him, “I’m never going to talk to you again.”

You can also use direct speech in the past and future tenses. You’ll often come across direct speech in the past tense to describe something that has already happened. Most written forms of English will use direct speech in this way.

Indirect speech is used to report what someone may have said, and so it is always used in the past tense. Instead of using inverted commas, we can show that someone’s speech is being described by using the word “that” to introduce the statement first.

For example:

“She said that she was not hungry.”

You can use different verbs to express what is being reported – while “to say” is quite commonly used, you might also want to use “to tell”, to describe something that has been told to you.

28. Buatlah 2 contoh kalimat direct speech and indirect speech yg simple present tense, present continous tense, perfect tense, future tense


Halo kak, saya bantu jawab ya.

1) Simple present tense :

Direct : He said to me, "I am ready."
Indirect : He told me he was ready.

Direct : She said, "I don't like cucumbers."
Indirect : She said she didn't like cucumbers.

2) Present continuous tense :

Direct : She said to us, "I'm cooking."
Indirect : She told us she was cooking.

Direct : My mother said to me, "Are you studying?"
Indirect : My mother asked me if I was studying.

3) Present perfect tense :

Direct : She said, "I have eaten."
Indirect : She said she had eaten.

Direct : He said to us, "Have you finished?"
Indirect : He asked us if we had finished.

4) Future simple tense :

Direct : You said, "I will eat tonight."
Indirect : You said you would eat that night.

Direct : They said to me, "We aren't going to hurt you."
Indirect : They told me they weren't going to hurt me.

35. kak bantu jawab pliss. ubahlah kalimat diatas dari inderect speech menjadi direct speech!!!​


DIRECT-INDIRECT SPEECH

3. Sonia said that her sister was sleeping.

➡ Sonia said, "My sister is sleeping."

4. Ani said that they were reading a novel.

➡ Ani said, "We are reading a novel."

5. Helen said that she wanted to buy an umbrella.

➡ Helen said, "I want to buy an umbrella."

6. Simin said that he would call me.

➡ Simin said, "I will call you."

7. Laura said that she had eaten a banana.

➡ Laura said, "I have eaten a banana."

8. Jono asked Jini what she was reading.

➡ Jono said to Jini, "What are you reading?"

9. Gina told me to move quickly.

➡ Gina said to me, "Move quickly!."

10. Lucy asked me why I hated rats.

➡ Lucy said to me, "Why do you hate rats?"

EXPLANATION

Reported speech/indirect speech adalah pelaporan ulang apa yang telah disampaikan narasumber kepada orang lain tanpa mengubah informasi tersirat di jeda waktu yang berbeda.

Cara mengubah Indirect Speech ke Direct Speech

1) Gunakan reporting verb yang sesuai dengan situasinya (say, tell, told, asked)

2) Gunakan tanda petik ganda dan tanda huruf lainnya untuk menandai kalimat petikannya

3) Kalimat petikan selalu diawali dengan huruf kapital

4) Rubahlah tense masing² kalimat ketika kata pengiringnya (reporting verb) menggunakan bentuk past tense. Pada umumnya kata kerja klausa kata bendanya mengalami pergeseran (backshift); baik dari keterangan waktu, petunjuk maupun tenses.

~ Past tense menjadi present tense

~ Past perfect menjadi present perfect/past tense

~ the previous day menjadi yesterday

5) Perhatikan bentuk kalimatnya; apakah termasuk afirmasi, tanya dan imperatif.

6) Gunakan sudut pandang dalam merubah pronomina (kata ganti orang)

Contoh:

I.D.S : Lucy asked me why I hated rats.

D.S : Lucy said to me, "Why do you hate rats?"

Indiirect speech-nya termasuk jenis WH-question (kalimat tanya WH-words) dan kata pengiringnya berbentuk "past." Jadi, noun clause verb-nya mengalami perubahan tenses satu tahap dari past tense ke present tense.

Menggunakan pronomina persona kedua berdasarkan sudut pandang & posisi si pewarta.
