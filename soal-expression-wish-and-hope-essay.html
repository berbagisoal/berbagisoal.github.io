6. cari perbedaan expression of hope and wish beserta contohnya(5)​


Hope:

1. Hope merupakan ekspresi seseorang yang menginginkan sesuatu untuk terjadi dan harapan itu bisa diwujudkan dan sangat mungkin terjadi.  

2. Hope bisa digunakan untuk merujuk pada harapan di masa lalu, sekarang, ataupun masa depan. Pada umumnya, hope digunakan untuk merujuk harapan saat ini atau yang akan terjadi di masa yang akan datang.

Contohnya:

-To say what you hope about the past, you use "simple past"

I hope she found the new pet (aku harap dia menemukan peliharaan baru)

-To say what you hope about the present, you use "simple present"

I hope he is all right (aku harap dia baik-baik saja)

-To say what you hope about the future, you use "simple present/future"

I hope he has a good time in New York next month (aku harap dia akan bersenang-senang di New York bulan depan)

We have to hope and pray (that) the operation will go well (kita harus berharap dan berdoa agar operasi itu berjalan dengan baik)

I hope I will get an A because I have studied hard (aku harap aku akan mendapatkan nilai A karena aku sudah belajar keras)

I hope my team will win (aku harap timku akan menang)

____________________

____________________

Wish:

1. Wish merupakan ekspresi seseorang mengharapkan sesuatu yang kenyataannya tidak bisa terjadi atau tidak sesuai dengan apa yang diharapkan .

2. Wish lebih sering merujuk pada keinginan yang tidak terwujud di masa lampau, biasanya berupa penyesalan.

3. Wish juga digunakan untuk memberikan ucapan/ungkapan untuk harapan pada seseorang di masa yang akan datang untuk suskes, bahagia atau hari raya.

Contohnya:  

-To say what you wish about the past, you use "past perfect"

I wish I had passed my ESL class last semester (aku berharap aku lulus kelas ESL semester lalu. Faktanya aku tidak lulus)

-To say what you wish about the present, you use "simple past"

I wish I had a million dollars (aku berharap punya satu juta dolar. Faktanya aku tidak punya uang sebanyak itu)

-To say what you wish about the future, you use "would"

I wish Clinton would do more to help the poor (aku berharap Clinton akan lebih banyak membantu orang miskin. Faktanya Clinton tidak pernah membantu orang miskin)

-To give an expression of someone's success, or big day/events

I wish you a safe journey (Semoga perjalananmu aman)

Wish you all the best (semoga yg terbaik selalu untukmu)

We wish you a merry Christmas (Kami mengucapkan selamat Natal)

We wish you a happy Eid-Mubarak (Kami mengucapkan selamat Hari Raya Idul Fitri)

13. Buatlah pertanyaan essay tentang hope and wish


What is the difference between hope and wish? Don’t both of them speak about things in the future?
They do, but they need different grammar patterns, and the patterns tell us about how probable the speaker is about their coming true.
A wish is something that you desire to come true but it has no basis in reality. It's passive and can lead to frustration and even depression. Hoping, on the other hand, is having a desire with real expectation

18. Berikan masing-masing 3 contoh dialog expression of congratulation, hope and wish!​


Jawaban:

Expressing congratulations, hope, and wish

John: Congratulations, Sue.

Sue: Thank you, but, what for?

John: For winning the art competition. You did win, didn't you?

Sue: No, I didn't, or more correctly, I haven't. The announcement is not until next month.

John: Oh, dear. Silly me. What's tonight's party for, then?

Sue: Oh. It's my little sister birthday. She's turning five.

John: I wish her a happy birthday, then.

Sue: Thank you. I'm sure she appreciate it.

John: I like your sister. She's very cute. I hope she'll have a great birthday party.

Sue: Wait. Aren't you coming?

John: No, I'm not. I have a deadline for tomorrow. I'll be burning the midnight oil tonight.

Sue: Oh. I hope you can finish your deadline on time. Good luck, John.

John: Thanks.

"Congratulations" is the word we often use when we want to congratulate someone on their achievements.

The word "congratulations" itself can stand alone as an interjection or we can elaborate it using phrases like "for your achievement" or "on your getting a new job", etc.

Other expressions to use to congratulate someone:

1. I knew you can do it.

2. I'm so happy for you.

3. May I congratulate you on…  

4. I must congratulate you on...

5. Please accept my sincerest congratulations

6. etc.

When you want to wish someone something, we can use expressions using "S+wish+someone+interjection"

e.g.

1. I wish you a happy new year.

2. He wishes her good luck.

Or we can just say the interjection.

e.g.

1. Good luck!

2. Happy birthday!

3. Godspeed!

When expressing hope for the future, we can use the construction "S+hope+present simple/present future".

e.g.

1. I hope Indonesia wins the World Cup someday.

2. We hope he will come back safely.

3. etc.

Expressing hope

I hope the bus comes soon. Saya berharap bus datang segera.

Responding hope

Tidak hanya contoh kalimat wish and hope saja yang penting untuk diketahui namun respons yang diberikan ini pun wajib untuk diketahui. Saat Anda mendapatkan atau mendengar kalimat hope dari seseorang ada beberapa jawaban yang bisa Anda gunakan seperti berikut ini:

Expressing wish

I wish that I had a luxury big house. Saya berharap memiliki rumah besar yang mewah.

40. bagaimana cara mengungkapkan expression of hope and wish? (tuliskan polanya)​
