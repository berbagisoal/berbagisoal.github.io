23. berilah contoh kalimat present tense, present continous tense, present perfect tense, & present perfect continous tense yang tergolong passive voice


1. present tense (s+be(is,am,are)+v3+by+o
  ex: he paints the wall (active)
       the wall is painted by him
  (passive)

2. present continous (s+be+being+v3+by+o)
ex:
she is taking some chalks in the box
some chalks is being taken by her in the box

3. present perfect tense ( s+has/have+been+v3+by+o)
ex: 
we have collected the stamps for two months
the stamps have been collected by us for two months

27. Tenses simple present tensepresent continous tensepresent perfect tensepast continous tensepast perfect tensefuture tensefuture continous tensetolong bantu jawab rumusnya​


Subject + V1 (s/es) + objectbe (am/are/is) + subject + verb1 -ing + complementSubject + Have/Has + Verb-3.Subject + was / were + verb1 + ing + complement SubjectS+had+Past Participle (Verb3).Will/Shall + Subject + Verb.subject + will/shall + be +verb-ing.

1. pada simple present tense

rumus:. [ s + to Be (is, am, are) + Verb Bentuk III + by + O]

2. pada present continuous tense

rumus: [ S + is am are + being + Verb Bentuk III + by + O]

3. pada present perfect tense

rumus: [ S + Have/Has + been + Verb Bentuk III + O]

4. pada past continuous tense

rumus: [ S + Was/Were + being + Verb Bentuk III + by + O]

5. pada past perfect tense

rumus: [ S + Had + been + Verb Bentuk III + by + O]

39. Apa rumus dari present continous tense dan past continous tense

Past continous tense=S + be (was/were) + present participle (v-ing)
