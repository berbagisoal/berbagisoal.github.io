Contoh Soal Obligation:

Pilih ekspresi yang tepat untuk mengekspresikan obligation dalam kalimat berikut:

a. You ________ brush your teeth twice a day to maintain good oral hygiene. (should / could)

b. We ________ finish this project by the deadline, or we will face penalties. (must / might)

c. To enter the building, you ________ show your ID card to the security officer. (should / have to)

Jawaban:

a. You should brush your teeth twice a day to maintain good oral hygiene.

b. We must finish this project by the deadline, or we will face penalties.

c. To enter the building, you have to show your ID card to the security officer.

Contoh Soal Suggestion:

Pilih ekspresi yang tepat untuk mengekspresikan suggestion dalam kalimat berikut:

a. I think you ________ take a break to relax your mind. (must / might)

b. If you're bored, you ________ try reading a book or doing a crossword puzzle. (could / should)

c. Sam isn't sure where to go on vacation. ________ visiting the beach or exploring a new city? (How about / Must)

Jawaban:

a. I think you might take a break to relax your mind.

b. If you're bored, you could try reading a book or doing a crossword puzzle.

c. Sam isn't sure where to go on vacation. How about visiting the beach or exploring a new city?

4. apa bedanya obligation dan suggestion?


Answer:

Those two terms, obligation and suggestion are quite different in meaning and expression. Obligation means something that you must do while suggestion means a new or different idea that you can suggest to others. Here are the expression of each of them:

Obligation: - you must/ have to + V1

- you are obliged to + V1

- you need to + V1

Suggestion: - let's + V1

- shall / why don't we + V1?

- how / what about + Ving?

Kelas: SMP

Mapel: English

Kategori: expression

Obligation: Kewajiban. Obligation is something that must be done with a full sense of responsibility

Rules: Aturan. Rule is a provision made to limit behavior or bind a person or society

Suggestion: Saran. Suggestion is a solution aimed at resolving the problem at hand

23. tulis 5 soal dialog tentang suggestion dan 5 soal dialog tentang obligation! (Dalam bahasa Inggris)


Jawaban:

 Contoh Dialog Expressing Obligation Bahasa Inggris – Expressing obligation merupakan ungkapan yang menyatakan suatu keharusan. Dalam bahasa Inggris ada beberapa ungkapan yang dapat digunakan untuk mengungkapkan suatu keharusan, seperti:

–    It is an obligation…

–    It is compulsion…

–    Must

–    Have to

–    Ought to

–    Should

–    etc.

Contoh Dialog Expressing Obligation Bahasa Inggris:

Contoh 1 :

Dini: Hy Sita!

(Hai Sita!)

Sita: Hy Dini.

(Hai Dini.)

Dini: You look sad. Is something bad happen?

(Kamu terlihat sedih. Apa ada hal buruk yang terjadi?)

Sita: Yes. I feel disappointed with myself.

(Ya. Aku merasa kecewa pada diriku sendiri.)

Dini: Why?

(Kenapa?)

Sita: Here! Look at my examination result. It is bad.

(Ini! Lihatlah hasil ujian milikku. Hasilnya buruk.)

Dini: Don’t worry. You can do well on the next examination.

(Jangan khawatir. Kamu bisa melakukannya lebih baik pada ujian berikutnya.)

Sita: I don’t know. I am not sure.

(Entahlah. Aku tidak yakin.)

Dini: One thing that you must do is to study even harder than you did before. I know you can do it.

(Satu hal yang harus kamu lakukan adalah belajar lebih giat lagi daripada sebelumnya. Aku tahu kamu bisa melakukannya.)

Sita: Thank’s Dini.

(Terima kasih Dini.)

29. 10 contoh soal dan jawaban tentang giving suggestion (dalam bahasa inggris)


Giving suggestion (memberikan saran) diucapkan oleh seseorang kepada lawan bicaranya dengan tujuan untuk menyampaikan saran atau masukan kepada lawan bicaranya, agar orang tersebut bersedia melaksanakan saran atau masukannya. Saran atau suggestion yang diberikan ini bisa berupa nasihat, pendapat, gagasan, dan yang semacamnya.

Beberapa contoh ekspresi yang sering digunakan untuk giving suggestion di antaranya adalah:

I think .......

What about .............

What if ....................

You should ....................

Let's ..............................

Maybe you want to ............................

How if .......

How about .....

Contoh 10 pertanyaan pilihan ganda tentang giving suggestion.

1. Tom looks very tired. What's your suggestion to him?

a. you should eat more.

b. you should study harder

c. I think you ought to get some rest

d. what if you go for a walk?

jawaban: c

2. Linda is working on her homework, but she finds some difficult questions. What will you suggest her?

a. I think you should stop studying.

b. maybe you can sleep now.

c. What if you drink to refresh yourself?

d. what about going to your friend's house to study together?

jawaban: d

3. A: .............................. go to the cinema and watch a good film?

B: That's a good idea

a. you must

b. you have

c. I think you should

d. what if we

jawaban: d

4. A: I feel drowsy.

B: ........................................

a. you should take a rest.

b. what if you study harder?

c. what about calling your friend for help?

d. you had better call the police.

jawaban: a

5. This is Saturday night. What will you suggest your friend?

a. I think we should go to the town square for sight seeing.

b. what about going to the doctor?

c. you should speak to your teacher.

d. what if we take this to be repaired?

jawaban: a

6. Doctor: ...................... smoking if you don't want to get sick.

Patient: Yes sir, I will try.

a. what if you stop

b. you should stop

c. how about

d. maybe you can stop

jawaban: b

7. A: I am so hungry.

B: .......................................

a. I think you should take a rest.

b. You ought to read it more carefully.

c. Calm down! You should be more relaxed.

d. What about going to a restaurant?

jawaban: d

8. Mother: I want to cook an omelette.

You: ..............................

a. Let's buy some eggs.

b. Let's buy some syrup.

c. I think you should buy a ticket first.

d. I think you should go to a barbershop.

jawaban: a

9. Your friend needs a book for his homework. But he doesn't have enough money to buy it. What will you suggest him?

a. Maybe you can borrow from our seniors?

b. I think you shouldn't do the homework.

c. What if we go to the zoo?

d. You ought to get some rest.

jawaban: a

10. You and your friends are on a trip to a beach in Banyuwangi. However, none of you knows about the route the that beach. What will be your suggestion?

a. I think we can go home now.

b. What about going to a restaurant?

c. Maybe you can open Google Maps application on your phone.

d. You should read the instruction more carefully.

40. contoh obligation , suggestion , prohibition


Jawaban:

Contoh Obligation

I must talk to my mother before I leave. (Saya harus berbicara pada ibu saya sebelum saya berangkat)

You must leave now. (Anda harus berangkat sekarang)

Contoh Suggestion

Let's go to the bookstore this weekend! (Ayo pergi ke toko buku akhir pekan ini!)

Why don't we play Taylor Swift's new song? (Mengapa kita tidak memutar lagu baru Taylor Swift?)

Contoh Prohibiton

Don't lie. : jangan berbohong

Don't cheat. : jangan mencontek

Penjelasan:

Obligation adalah  obligation berarti keharusan. Untuk mengungkapkan ide dari sebuah keharusan, berbicara tentang aturan, kewajiban, atau untuk memberikan nasihat yang tegas pada orang lain atau pada diri sendiri.

Suggestion adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan bagaimana cara meminta dan memberi saran kepada orang lain.

Prohibition adalah ungkapan untuk memperingatkan orang lain agar tidak melakukan sesuatu atau melarang.
